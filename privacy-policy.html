Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 28th april 2025

Welcome to Transmate.pro! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our browser extension.

Information We Collect

We value your privacy. Transmate.pro does not collect, store, or share your personal information, messages, or browsing history. The extension processes translation data locally or through secure APIs without saving your data on our servers.

How We Use Information

Our extension uses your input or selected text solely for the purpose of translating it and improving user experience. No input data is permanently stored or used for any other purpose.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party APIs (such as AI translation engines) to provide high-quality translation services. These services may process your text temporarily for translation purposes, following their own privacy policies.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, please note that no transmission of data over the internet is completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective when posted on this page.
